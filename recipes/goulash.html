Goulash Recipe

Description

Goulash is a stew or soup that originated in Hungary and is made with meat, vegetables, and paprika. It's a national dish of Hungary and is commonly eaten in Central Europe.

Ingredients

  • Elbow Pasta
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onions
  • Bell Peppers
  • Dices Tomatoes
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Sliced American Cheese
  • Paprika
  • Garlic
  • Seasoned Salt

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef along with the onion and bell pepper. Drain any fat and then toss in garlic until fragrant.
  2. Pour in tomato sauce and diced tomatoes along with beef broth and Worcestershire sauce for some added depth of flavor
  3. Add in seasonings as well as dried macaroni noodles and let simmer until the noodles are cooked through.
  4. Finally stir in the cheddar cheese and dig in!
